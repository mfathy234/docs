#    Scrum Team
****
  
**Cross-Functional**  
Scrum Teams are cross-functional, meaning members collectively possess all the skills needed to deliver value every sprint.

**Self-Managing**  
Teams are self-managing, internally deciding who does what, when, and how without external assignment.

**Size: 10 Members or Fewer**  
Typically composed of 10 or fewer members. Smaller teams tend to communicate more effectively and achieve higher productivity.

**Scaled Scrum**  
When a team grows too large, it should be divided into multiple cohesive Scrum Teams. These teams share the same product goal, product backlog, and product owner to maintain alignment.

**Responsibility**  
The Scrum Team is responsible for all product-related activities, including stakeholder collaboration, maintenance, operations, development, and any other necessary tasks.

**Accountability**  
The entire Scrum Team is collectively accountable for delivering a valuable and usable product increment each sprint.
