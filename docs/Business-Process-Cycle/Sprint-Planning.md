
Each **Squad** has its own **Sprint Planning** session based on the **User Stories assigned to that team**.

### 1. Define the Sprint Goal

*   The **Business team** explains the overall sprint objective that all teams will work towards, ensuring a clear and shared vision.
    

### 2. Review User Stories planned for the Sprint

*   Each team reviews its assigned User Stories and ensures they are clear and well understood.
    

### 3. Capacity Check

*   The team confirms that it has enough capacity and resources to deliver the planned work within the sprint duration.
    

### 4. Identify Risks and Dependencies

*   Any risks or dependencies between teams that may impact delivery are discussed, and mitigation plans are defined.
    

### 5. Task Breakdown and Estimation

*   If the User Stories are clear, the team breaks them down into tasks.
    
*   All team members participate in creating the tasks and defining the **estimated time** for each task.
    

### 6. Meeting Duration

*   The maximum duration of the **Sprint Planning** session is **4 hours**.
