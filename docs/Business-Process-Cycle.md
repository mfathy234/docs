1.  **Requirements Collection**  
    The business team submits all required business needs and clarifications.
    
2.  **UX Design Request**  
    The business team requests the necessary screens and user experience designs from the UX team.
    
3.  **UX Review**  
    The business team reviews the UX outputs and confirms alignment with the requirements.
    
4.  **User Story Preparation**  
    The business team documents the user stories with full details and acceptance criteria.
    
5.  **Product Approval**  
    The product team reviews and approves both the UX designs and the user stories.
    
6.  **Grooming Session**  
    Before the Sprint Planning meeting, the business team explains the user stories to the tech leads, and the tech leads provide initial time estimates for the related tasks.
    
7.   **Test Case Creation**  
    The QC team prepares the required test cases based on the approved user stories.
    
8.  **Pre-Planning Review**  
    All team members review the user stories one day before the planning meeting and add their comments or clarifications.
    
9.  **Sprint Planning Discussion**  
    During the planning meeting, the business team responds to all comments and clarifies any open points.
    
10.  **Task Assignment**  
    Tech leads discuss the estimated effort with the developers and begin assigning the tasks accordingly.
