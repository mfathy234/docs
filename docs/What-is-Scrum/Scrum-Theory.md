# Empiricism

Empiricism emphasizes learning through direct experience. Knowledge is gained through careful observation and practical engagement rather than relying solely on theoretical assumptions.

# Lean Thinking

Lean Thinking focuses on essentials by systematically eliminating waste and prioritizing activities that add real value for customers.

# Why Scrum Theory Matters

*   **Continuous Learning Culture**  
    Fosters an environment where teams actively pursue improvement and view change as an opportunity for growth.
    
*   **Risk Reduction**  
    Enhances visibility and manageability of work, enabling early identification of potential issues before they escalate into costly problems.
    
*   **Stakeholder Alignment**  
    Brings teams and stakeholders together around common goals, priorities, and transparent tracking of progress.
